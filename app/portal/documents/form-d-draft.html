STATE NOTICE (BLUE SKY) REQUIREMENTS

[DRAFT - ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED: State notice requirements vary by jurisdiction and change periodically. Counsel must verify current requirements.]
IMPORTANT: In addition to filing Form D with the SEC, issuers conducting Rule 506(c) offerings must also comply with state securities laws ("Blue Sky" laws). This typically involves filing Form D or a state-specific notice form with state securities regulators and paying applicable fees.

States Where Notice Filing is Required

Notice filings are generally required in states where:

  • The issuer's principal place of business is located
  • Any purchaser of securities resides
  • Any sales were made or offers directed

Amendments to Form D

An amendment to Form D must be filed for any of the following reasons:

  • To correct a material mistake of fact or error in the previously filed notice
  • To reflect a change in the information provided in the previously filed notice (with certain exceptions)
  • Annually, on or before the first anniversary of the most recent previously filed notice, if the offering is continuing
Set calendar reminder for annual amendment deadline
